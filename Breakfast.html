The Priority of Breakfast

Sizzling Monday

Start your week with a sizzling plate of bacon, eggs, toast, and breakfast potatoes.

Fluffy Tuesday

Indulge in a stack or two of fluffy pancakes alongside eggs, sausages, toast, and breakfast potatoes to start a well-rested, comfortable Tuesday!

Golden Wednesday

Dive into some fresh golden waffles served with eggs, Canadian bacon, toast, and breakfast potatoes as a familiar staple to brighten up your midweek morning!

Temptation Thursday

Give into your Thursday temptations with a variety spread of donuts, eggs, bacon, toast, and potato pancakes. A delectable twist to your Thursday morning!

Fusion Friday

Experience a fusion of flavors with another variety spread of pasteries, eggs, sausage, toast, fried apples, and homestyle potatoes. The perfect start to this releasing Friday dawn!

Savory Saturday

Savor the flavor with the usual eggs, bacon, sausage, with the surprise of sausage gravy, hash brown casserole, fried apples, and buscuits.

Blessful Sunday

have a blessed Sunday with a spread of waffles, donuts, assorted pasteries, eggs, and bacon with the chef's choice of breakfast potatoes, toast, and cereal. A leisure end to the exciting week.

Drink Up!

Enjoy a selection of freshly brewed coffee, tea, or other rereshing drinks such as milk and a variety of juices to compliment your breakfast and end that breakfast with a refreshing note!
